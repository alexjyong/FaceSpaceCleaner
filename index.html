About
Fork off of fb-delete from https://github.com/spieglt/fb-delete

This will pernamently delete the selected data. (Well, as best as you can "delete" anything from Facebook really. It probably exists still somewhere. üòä)

Absolutely none of your data is sent to me or anywhere other than Facebook's servers. (The program is open source, look at the code if you don't believe me.)

Be sure to get a backup of your FB data from here: https://www.facebook.com/help/1701730696756992, just in case you accidently delete your treasured photos.

Use this app at your own risk. There is no promise that this isn't bug free. By using this, you accept that I am not responsible for deletion of anything you didn't want deleted. üòá
